Erin Chacon, Designer & Maker of Things

About

Once I knew that I couldn't be a dog when I grew up, I decided to be an artist. (Can't join 'em? Then draw 'em!) I gravitate towards wacky characters and animals as subjects, along with a healthy dose of vintage style. My love of steampunk is why I also journeyed into web design. I love seeing how things work, with a strong emphasis on aesthetics. With my bold linework and colors, my goal in everything I do is to make people smile. I also enjoy lifting others up. Whether it is through eyecatching merchandise or a well designed webpage, I love collaboration. If my work helps you acheive your goals of being seen in the world, I'm a happy camper!


I'm a nonbinary Arizona-based illustrator and aspiring web designer. Since 2015, I have traveled nationwide to showcase my work and peddle my wares at various conventions and events. With my freelance business, Strawberrygears Art, I've produced merchandise for influencers, and designed branding and characters for small productions. When I'm not drawing or coding, I'm making costumes, playing board games, or fighting the urge to adopt more cats.
